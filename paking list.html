Packing List

Personal Essentials

  • Wallet
  • Keys
  • Phone
  • Sunglasses
  • Hat

Weather-Appropriate Clothing

  • Jacket or sweater (depending on temperature)
  • Scarf/gloves (if cold)
  • Comfortable shoes (sneakers, hiking boots)
  • Rain gear (umbrella, raincoat)

Water and Snacks

  • Water bottle (reusable)
  • Snacks (fruits, energy bars, nuts)

Safety and Comfort Items

  • Face Mask
  • Hand Sanitizer
  • First Aid Kit (basic items like band-aids, antiseptic wipes)

Optional Items

  • Backpack/small bag (to carry everything)
  • Book or Notebook (for downtime)
  • Camera (for photos)
  • Portable Charger (to keep your phone powered)
