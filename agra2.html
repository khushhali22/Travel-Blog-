ABOUT AGRA

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world. It is located in the city of Agra in northern India. A ruler named Shah Jahan had the Taj Mahal built as a monument and tomb for his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Shah Jahan was the Muslim ruler of the Mughal Empire in India from 1628 to 1658.

Moti Masjid

The Moti Masjid is a 17th-century mosque located within the Agra. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, the mosque is made entirely of white marble.

Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri is located in Agra District in the State of Uttar Pradesh in northern India. It was constructed southeast of an artificial lake, on the slopping levels of the outcrops of the Vindhyan hill ranges. Known as the “city of victory”, it was made capital by the Mughal emperor Akbar
