Museums

The Louvre is a museum in Paris. It has millions of visitors every year because of its art collection. It is the most visited art museum in the world. The most famous piece of art in the Louvre is the Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a famous French landmark. It is a wrought-iron tower known all over the world for its beautiful architecture and cultural significance. It's about the same height as an 81-storey building, standing at 324 m from the base to the tip.
