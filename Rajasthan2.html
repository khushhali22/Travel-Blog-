ABOUT RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan

The State of Rajasthan was formed on 30 March 1949 when the states of the Rajputana Agency of the erstwhile British Empire in India were merged into the new Indian Union. Its capital and largest city is Jaipur. Other important cities are Jodhpur, Kota, Bikaner, Ajmer, Bharatpur and Udaipur.

Jaipur

Jaipur was India's first planned city. This is reflected in the well-designed intersections and straight, wide main streets. Just 260 km (162 miles) from the capital New Delhi, Jaipur is a busy city with a population of over 3 million people.

Jodpur

Jodhpur commonly recognized as the Sun City because of the bright sunny days throughout the year. Being the second largest city of the colorful land of Rajasthan, this Sun City with its blue colored walls and buildings is better known as the Blue City as well.
