ABOUT CANADA

Canada

Canada is famous for the gorgeous scenery and uninhabited land. The views of the untouched and natural environment are breathtaking and composed of beautiful lakes and rivers.

Toronto

Located on the north shore of Lake Ontario, Toronto is Canada's most populated city and the capital of the province of Ontario. The city is Canada's primary financial and commercial center. It is home to the Toronto Stock Exchange and many large businesses. Toronto also is a major international trading center.
